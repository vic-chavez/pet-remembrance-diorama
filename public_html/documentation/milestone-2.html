Content Strategy

My website will be one page and consist of the following areas:

Overview: This area will have 3 sections. The first section will start with a title bar and a logo. The next section will have a nav bar showcasing the links available for this website. The final section of this area will showcase a Jumbotron with a mission statement and a picture of our final product.

How It Works:This area is showcasing the entire process from start to finish in a card layout. Each card will have a title, picture and text explaining the procedure. There are a total of 6 cards. In addition, there will be a series of arrows showcasing the direction of travel.

Gallery: This area is a showcasing of the completed projects in a gallery format. There are a total of 6 cards. Each card will have a picture with an explanation of what each graphic represents.

Contact:This is our final area. It has 3 sections. The first section is a map and contact information. The next section is a contact form in which the customer can reach out via email and a message. The final section is the external links to Github and LinkedIn.
