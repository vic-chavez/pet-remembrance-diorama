HOW IT WORKS

Gather Pictures

This is the beginning of the journey where we gather usable pictures for printing.

Manipulate Image

First we convert your picture to an .stl using a website; https://imagetostl.com/.

Next we use a program called Chitubox to scale, adjust, and slice your image into a usable printing file.

Print/Clean/Cure 3D Sculpture

There are 3 stages to creating a print.

First we print using our resin 3D printer.

Second, we clean the prints using an ultrasonic cleaner.

Third, we cure the print using UV technology.

Prep and Paint 3D Sculpture

First, we prime the sculpture using a primer.

Next we smooth out any rough edges.

Our final step is to apply the paint.

Build Diorama

At this stage we build your custom Diorama scene; whether it be a single portrait, a portrait set on a backdrop, or a mobile.

Deliver to Customer

This is our final step in our process where we deliver our product to you. We hope you enjoy it as much as we have enjoyed making it.
